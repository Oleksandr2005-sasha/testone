Solar Energy

Solar energy harnesses the power of the sun to generate electricity or heat. It is a clean and renewable energy source.

Wind Energy

Wind energy uses turbines to convert the kinetic energy of wind into electrical power. It is one of the fastest-growing energy technologies.

Hydropower

Hydropower, or water power, uses the energy of flowing water to produce electricity. It is a reliable and sustainable energy source.
